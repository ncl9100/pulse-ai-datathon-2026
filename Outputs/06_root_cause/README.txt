ROOT CAUSE DIAGNOSIS
=====================
For the 85+ worst-performing projects, identifies the primary cause of loss.

  cause_effect_diagnosis : one row per poor performer; primary_cause label + cost overrun
  chain_a_*              : projects hit by design rework losses
  chain_b_*              : projects hit by material shortage / OT standby
  chain_c_*              : projects where RFI cost impacts went unrecovered
  chain_d_*              : projects with large rejected CO values
  chain_e_*              : projects with high early-phase CO volume (leading indicator)

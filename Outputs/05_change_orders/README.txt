05_CHANGE_ORDERS
================

Change order revenue, rejection rates, RFI-to-CO recovery, and submission timing.
  co_analysis_by_project.csv      — Approved revenue, rejected losses, schedule impact per project
  co_analysis_by_gc.csv           — Rejection rates and dollar losses broken down by GC
  co_rfi_recovery.csv             — Cost-impact RFIs matched to approved COs;
                                    unmatched RFIs = unrecovered cost
  co_timing.csv                   — CO count by project stage (Early/Mid/Late/Closeout)
